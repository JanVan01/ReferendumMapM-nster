Referendum Map Münster

Welcome to the Referendum Map Münster. Here you will be provided with infomation regarding the recent referendum that took place in Münster on November 6th in 2016. The referendum took place to detect wehther some shops should be open on three additional Sundays or be closed. The affected shops are those in the two main shopping streets in the inner city (Salzstraße and Prinzipalmarkt).

This Application provides a visual representation of the results from the referendum devided according to the different voting districts. The coloured districts showing the overall result for the particular district. By zooming in and out the user will be provided with more or less voting districts. By clicking on the districts additional information are shown, like the number of Yes- and No-votes.

The Project

This Application was part of a project that was carried out as part of a course that took place at the Institut for Geoinformatics. The course is part of the master study programm "Geoinformatics". The course "Geoinforamtion in Society" was about turning existing data to geographic linked open data and build a software application which consumes the data and visualize it.
